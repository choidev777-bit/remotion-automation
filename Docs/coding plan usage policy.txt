# Usage Policy

The GLM Coding Plan is a subscription package designed specifically for AI-powered coding. With a minimal investment, you can enjoy Zai’s high-intelligence models across mainstream AI coding tools, delivering an intelligent, fast, and stable coding experience.

## <Icon icon="bolt" iconType="solid" color="#ffffff" size={30} />  Rate Limits

Rate (concurrency) limits are tied to your plan tier. The platform dynamically adjusts these limits based on resource availability, with the general principle being **Max > Pro > Lite**.

For each project, you can use methods like Subagent to make concurrent model calls. Our recommended number of concurrent projects is as follows:

* **Lite**: Recommended for developing a single project at a time
* **Pro**: Recommended for developing 1–2 projects simultaneously
* **Max**: Recommended for developing 2 or more projects simultaneously

<Tip>
  Plan users will enjoy higher concurrency limits during off-peak hours (dynamically increased), enabling support for a greater number of concurrent projects.
</Tip>

## <Icon icon="users" iconType="solid" color="#ffffff" size={30} />  Account Usage Policy

<Warning>
  The **GLM Coding Plan** subscription is intended for **individual use by the subscriber**. If multiple users share the same account and subscription, it may be considered improper usage.

  In such cases, we may apply restrictions to the subscription benefits, and in severe cases the account itself may be affected.
</Warning>

<Note>
  The GLM Coding Plan is designed specifically for **Coding Scenarios**.

  If the system detects that the subscription is being used for requests clearly unrelated to coding scenarios, certain subscription benefits may be restricted to maintain fairness and platform stability. Once normal coding-related usage resumes, these restrictions may be automatically lifted.
</Note>

* To protect the rights of all subscribers, if your account violates the [Usage Rules](/legal-agreement/subscription-terms#4-usage-rules), and triggers risk control rules, it may be subject to risk control measures, including high-intensity throttling, account suspension, or permanent ban.
* If your account triggers a risk control rule, a notification may appear. You can [contact our support team](https://z.ai/contact) to request a review of the restriction.

<Danger>
  Violating the Usage Rules three or more times will result in an account ban.
</Danger>

## <Icon icon="dollar-sign" iconType="solid" color="#ffffff" size={30} />   Manage Your Subscription

You can manage your subscription, view billing details, and cancel the subscription as follows:

1. Log in to the Z.ai [API Platform](https://z.ai/subscribe?utm_source=zai\&utm_medium=index\&utm_term=glm-coding-plan\&utm_campaign=Platform_Ops&_channel_track_key=6lShUDnv).
2. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner → [Payment Method](https://z.ai/manage-apikey/billing).
3. In the left menu, select [Subscription](https://z.ai/manage-apikey/subscription).
4. To view billing history, go to Billing → [Billing History](https://z.ai/manage-apikey/billing).

## <Icon icon="arrows-rotate" iconType="solid" color="#ffffff" size={30} />   Subscription Period & Renewal

Your subscription will **automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle**, and the fee will be charged to your linked payment method.

The system will charge you in the following order:

1. Priority is given to the bonus balance in your platform account.
2. If the bonus balance is insufficient, the cash balance in your platform account will be used.
3. If the above balances are insufficient, the payment will be deducted from your linked third-party payment method (e.g., WeChat Pay, Alipay).

<Info>
  If you need to cancel your subscription, you can do so on the [Subscription](https://z.ai/manage-apikey/subscription) page.

  Please be sure to cancel **at least 3 days** before the next billing date to avoid automatic renewal.

  After cancellation, the current subscription period remains valid and will not be renewed upon expiration.
</Info>
